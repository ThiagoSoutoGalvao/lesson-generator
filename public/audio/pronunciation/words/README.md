# Word audio files

One MP3 per word, named by the word itself in lowercase (matches the `audio`
fields referenced across `soundCards.json`, `minimalPairs.json`, and
`edEndings.json`).

Examples: `ship.mp3`, `sheep.mp3`, `walked.mp3`, `wanted.mp3`

Native British English (RP) speaker, clear and consistent volume, short
real-word recordings. Sourced in Phase 2 — not committed yet.
